How to Play

  • This game is played by two players, with a respective set of 8 white or black 'pebbles' each.
  • In each player's turn, they can move one piece in any distance horizontally or vertically as long as they are not obstructed by another peice.
  • To move a piece, click on it to select it then click on a square to move it to that square.
  • Pieces can be captured if two of the opponent's pieces sandwich the piece horizontally or vertically.
  • You can, however, move your piece into a sandwiched square without it getting captured. It is only if your opponent moves the piece into a position to capture yours that your piece gets captured.
  • If your opponent is reduced to a ingle piece, then you win and the game ends.
  • If your opponent has more than one piece but cannot move any piece then you also win.
